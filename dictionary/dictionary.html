Abeam

On a line at right angles to the boat's length.

Aft

Towards the stern, or rearmost end, of the boat.

Ahead

In front of the bow, or frontmost end, of the boat.

Astern

Behind the stern, or rearmost end, of the boat.

Backstay

Runs from the stern to the top of the mast to support the mast. Part of the standing rigging.

Batten

A rod inserted in the leech, or rearmost edge, of a sail to support the sailcloth.

Batten Pocket

A pocket in the sail that holds the batten in place.

Beam

The widest line across the boat from right to left.

Beam Reach

When a boat travels at 90º to the oncoming wind, at which the boom is eased about halfway out. The fastest point of sail.
