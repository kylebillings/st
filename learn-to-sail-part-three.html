Sailing Theory for Beginners

Part III: Sailing Maneuvers

Welcome to part three of the Learn to Sail tutorial!

In part one, you learned about how sailboats are designed to derive power from the wind — windward push and leeward lift. In part II, you learned about how a sailboat’s direction is directly connected with the direction of the wind, and the names of every point of sail.

In part III, you’ll learn the maneuvers that sailors use to change direction, the knots you’ll need to know, and the final details that will make your first sail a ton of fun.
